About Pata Tours & Travels

We are a Tibetan-owned travel agency based in McLeod Ganj, Dharamshala, dedicated to offering authentic travel experiences across the Himalayan region. Rooted in genuine Tibetan hospitality, we combine professionalism with warmth to create journeys that connect you with the landscapes, spirituality, and cultural richness of the Himalayas.
